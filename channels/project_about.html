Overview

This page provides a quick overview of my life story.

  • Born and raised in New Jersey, I developed a passion for technology at an early age.
  • I played various sports throughout my school years, fostering teamwork and discipline.
  • These sports included basketball, track, and cross country, all the way through my senior year of high school.
  • After choosing the University of Vermont, I pursued a degree in Computer Science, where I honed my skills in programming and problem-solving.
  • I spent all four years as an active participant in my fraternity, Phi Mu Delta.
  • Through my involvement in the fraternity, I developed strong leadership and organizational skills, which have been invaluable in both my academic and professional endeavors.
  • I first served as VP of Member Development, then was elected President of the chapter. In my final semester, I served as VP of Administration to help mentor the next executive board.
  • In my free time, I still enjoy playing basketball, chess, video games, and spending time with my friends.
